# Bumble Breeze

🐝 **Bumble Breeze** is an autonomous companion robot designed to help protect vulnerable people during heat waves, especially seniors and young children.

## What It Does

- 🌡️ Monitors temperature in real time
- 👀 Detects human presence and motion
- 🌀 Activates a fan when heat and presence are detected together
- 🤖 Moves autonomously indoors to monitor its environment

## How We Built It

- Arduino-based embedded system
- Temperature and motion sensors
- Autonomous mobility with motors
- Fan controlled through a transistor/relay
- Threshold-based decision logic
- Cute and safe plastic body design

## Challenges

- Reliable detection with fewer false positives
- Indoor navigation in cluttered environments
- Power management and autonomy
- Activating cooling only when truly useful
- Building something robust enough for real-world care settings

## What We’re Proud Of

- ✅ A fully autonomous working prototype
- 💛 A friendly and non-threatening design
- 🏥 A health and well-being focused companion robot
- 📈 A concept adapted to a strong B2B market
